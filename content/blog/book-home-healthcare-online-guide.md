Booking home healthcare should not require endless phone calls or unclear follow-ups. With Ambimed, families across India can **choose a service, pick dates, and confirm care** — on the web or Android app.

## Why book online instead of by phone?

Digital booking gives you:

- **Instant availability** of services and rate plans
- **A clear record** of what you booked and when
- **Secure OTP sign-in** — no passwords to remember
- **Tracking and updates** once care begins

It also saves time for families coordinating care from another city or while at work.

## Step 1: Choose your service

Ambimed offers home healthcare across several categories:

- [Caregiver & attendant](/services/caregiver-assistant) — daily living support for elders
- [Home nurse](/services/home-nurse) — clinical nursing at home
- [Physiotherapy](/services/physiotherapy) — recovery and mobility sessions
- [Mother & baby care](/services/mother-baby) — support for new parents

Select the service that matches your loved one’s needs. If you are unsure, our team can guide you via the [contact section](/#contact).

## Step 2: Pick date and time

Choose a start date and preferred shift (for example, 10:00 AM – 12:00 PM or a 24-hour elder care plan). Longer bookings often have clearer monthly pricing — check our [pricing guide](/blog/transparent-home-healthcare-pricing-guide) for tips on comparing rates.

## Step 3: Confirm booking with OTP sign-in

Sign in with your mobile number and a one-time password. OTP login is:

- **Secure** — no shared passwords
- **Simple** — works on any phone
- **Fast** — confirm in under a minute

Once verified, review your booking summary and confirm. You will receive updates as your care plan is assigned.

## Step 4: Track your caregiver in the app

After booking, use the Ambimed Android app to:

- See your **assigned caregiver** and profile
- Get **visit updates** when care is in progress
- **Manage bookings** and view history

Tracking reduces anxiety for families — especially when you cannot be at home during every visit.

## Tips for a smooth first booking

- Have the patient’s basic details and address ready
- Note any mobility or medical requirements in advance
- Book early for post-discharge or festival-season demand
- Save the support number from your confirmation for quick help
