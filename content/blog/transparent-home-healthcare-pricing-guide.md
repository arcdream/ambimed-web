Home healthcare costs can feel opaque until a bill arrives. Families deserve to know exactly what they will pay — before care begins, not after.

## Why transparent pricing matters

When a loved one needs care at home, you are already managing emotion, logistics, and medical decisions. Unclear pricing adds unnecessary stress. Transparent providers share:

- **Clear daily rates** for each type of care
- **Upfront estimates** based on your service and duration
- **No hidden charges** in the final invoice

Ambimed was built around this principle: families should see real numbers from our catalogue and understand every line item.

## What affects your daily rate

Home healthcare pricing in India typically depends on:

- **Type of service** — caregiver, nurse, physiotherapist, or mother & baby care
- **Shift length** — 8-hour, 12-hour, or 24-hour coverage
- **Skill level** — basic attendant vs. trained nurse or specialist
- **Duration** — short-term recovery vs. long-term elder care

Always ask for a written estimate that lists each component rather than a single lump-sum quote.

## Red flags for hidden fees

Watch out for providers who:

- Refuse to share rates until after a home visit
- Add “administration” or “placement” fees without disclosure
- Bill for supplies or equipment you were not informed about
- Change rates mid-contract without written notice

A reputable agency will explain pricing on the website, in the app, or in a service estimate document you can review with your family.

## Tips for comparing providers fairly

When evaluating two or more home healthcare companies:

- Compare the **same shift type and duration** (apples to apples)
- Ask whether meals, travel, or equipment are included
- Check if holidays or overtime have different rates
- Confirm cancellation and replacement policies
